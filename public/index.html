CIT59x aims to complement the academic rigor of the MCIT program by promoting community and facilitating professional development for on-campus students.

Community

Students in the on-campus MCIT program come from countries around the globe. CIT59x aims to promote a sense of community and shared purpose among students.

Events

CIT59x regularly hosts events for on-campus MCIT students, including our annual 59xHackathon.

Professional Development

CIT59x supports student professional development through promoting networking opportunities with alumni and industry leaders, interview preparation, and more.
